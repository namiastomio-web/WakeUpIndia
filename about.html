About WakeUpIndia

WakeUpIndia is a public information website that tracks development work across Odisha using publicly available information.

WakeUpIndia is a citizzen driven paltform dedicated to promoting tansparency, Acccountability, and publi awareness about development projects across india. Our mission is to help people track government funded projects, monitor their progress, and encorage responsibility use of public money.

Every year, thousands of crores of rupees are allocated for roads, schools, hospitals, drinking water, electricity, bridges, and other public infrastructure. while many projects are completed successfully, some face delays,cost overruns, poor quality work, or remain unfinished. Citizens often struggle to access clear and organized informantion about these projects.

WakeUpIndia aims to bridge this gap by collecting publicly available information and presenting it in a simple, easy-to-understand format. Users can explore districts, view ongoing and completed development works, and stay informed about local progress. The platform also encourages citizens to report issues such as damaged roads, abandoned construction, poor-quality public works, delayed infrastructure projects, and other civic concerns. By increasing public participation and awareness, WakeUpIndia seeks to strengthen transparency and accountability.

WakeUpIndia does not target any political party or individual. It supports evidence-based discussion and responsible reporting. Any information about irregularities, corruption investigations, or misuse of public funds should be based on credible public records, official reports, audit findings, court proceedings, or verified news sources. Our vision is an India where every citizen can easily access information about public development, ask informed questions, and contribute to better governance through transparency and civic engagement.
